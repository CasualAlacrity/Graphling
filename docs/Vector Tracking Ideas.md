Risk: How much risk the pilot is willing to take on their runs
    - A Low Risk threshold might only trade within Stanton. 
    - A medium Risk threshold might be Stanton+Pyro. 
    - A High Risk threshold might be just Pyro

System Preference - Nyx, Pyro, Stanton - Can be associated with Risk. 
    - Trade Routes starting in Stanton and ending in Pyro or Nyx represent an amount of risk that isn't present in ST.

risk_tolerance{trade_risk, mining_risk}

trade_preference{profit, margin}: Whether the pilot leans more towards Profit or Margin in their trade choices.

mining_goal{quantity, quality, profit}: A user might prefer filling cargo fast, prioritize high quality, or profit-maxing
